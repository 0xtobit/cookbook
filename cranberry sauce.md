Ingredients
===========

* 2 cups firm washed cranberries
* 3/4 cup sour cream or Chobani plain yogurt
* 1 onion chopped 
* 1/2 cup sugar
* 2 tbsp horseradish

Instructions
============

Combine all ingredients in blender. Freeze overnight (or longer). Thaw at room
temperature before serving and refrigerate afterwards.
